Privacy Policy

How mobile information and messaging consent are handled.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

This policy applies to the Mike Schreiber Home Reminders SMS program operated by Mike Schreiber.

1. Messaging program

Mike Schreiber Home Reminders is a private household reminder program. Recipients enroll by texting START to the number displayed on the public SMS Signup page. Messages concern personal household routines, including dental retainer reminders.

2. Information collected

The program may process a recipient's mobile telephone number, SMS messages and replies, message timestamps, opt-in or opt-out status, reminder status, and limited delivery or troubleshooting records.

3. How information is used

Information is used only to enroll recipients, deliver requested reminders, process replies such as DONE, HELP, START, and STOP, prevent messages after opt-out, maintain limited reminder records, and troubleshoot service operation.

4. Mobile information and consent

We do not share, sell, rent, transfer, or otherwise provide mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, or messaging consent information to any third parties, affiliates, lead generators, or marketing organizations for marketing or promotional purposes.

We do not use mobile information or messaging consent for advertising, sales, affiliate marketing, lead generation, or third-party promotions.

5. Messaging disclosures

Message frequency varies and may include up to 3 messages per day while a reminder remains unconfirmed. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel or HELP for assistance.

6. Consent and enrollment

Enrollment is voluntary and occurs only when the recipient affirmatively texts START to the published program number after reviewing the disclosures on the SMS Signup page. No purchased lists, transferred consent, website checkboxes, verbal enrollment, or third-party enrollment methods are used.

7. Opting out

Recipients may withdraw consent at any time by replying STOP. No additional automated reminder messages will be sent after the opt-out is processed unless the recipient later enrolls again by texting START.

8. Retention and security

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary to operate the reminder program, honor consent and opt-out choices, maintain limited reminder history, and troubleshoot technical issues. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no telecommunications system can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Children and household use

The program is intended for one household and may be used by a parent to offer the service to a child. The recipient must still affirmatively enroll by texting START. The program is not offered to the general public and does not solicit unrelated children.

10. Contact

Questions may be directed to Mike Schreiber at mschrib@gmail.com.
