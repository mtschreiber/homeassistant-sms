Privacy Policy

Home Reminder Messaging

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This is a private, consent-based household reminder service. It is not used for advertising, sales, lead generation, or mass marketing.

1. Overview

Home Reminder Messaging is operated by Mike Schreiber for personal household use. The service sends automated SMS reminder and confirmation messages to household members who have agreed to receive them.

Examples may include reminders about retainers, household chores, appointments, routines, or other family responsibilities.

2. Information We Collect

The service may collect and process limited information, including:

  • A recipient's mobile telephone number.
  • Messages and replies sent through the reminder service.
  • The date and time a reminder or confirmation message was sent or received.
  • Reminder preferences, completion status, or requested reminder delays.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Send requested household reminder messages.
  • Receive and process replies such as DONE, HELP, or STOP.
  • Prevent additional reminders after a task is confirmed.
  • Maintain basic reminder history or completion records.
  • Troubleshoot and maintain the messaging service.

4. Mobile Information and Messaging Consent

Mobile information, text-messaging opt-in data, and consent will not be sold, rented, transferred, or shared with third parties, affiliates, lead generators, or marketing organizations for marketing or promotional purposes.

Mobile information may be processed by service providers that are necessary to deliver the messages, such as the telecommunications provider used to transmit SMS messages. Those providers receive only the information necessary to operate the messaging service.

5. No Advertising or Marketing

This service does not send promotional advertisements, sales offers, affiliate messages, or third-party marketing content.

6. Consent

Messages are sent only to household members who have knowingly agreed to receive automated reminder messages at the mobile number provided. Consent to receive messages is voluntary and may be withdrawn at any time.

7. Opting Out

A recipient may opt out at any time by replying STOP. After an opt-out request is received, no further automated reminder messages will be sent unless the recipient later provides renewed consent.

A recipient may reply HELP for assistance or contact the service operator using the email address below.

8. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably needed to operate the reminder service, document consent or opt-out requests, maintain reminder history, and troubleshoot technical problems.

9. Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect the limited information processed by this service. However, no internet-based or telecommunications system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's Privacy

This service may be used by a parent or legal guardian to send household reminders to their own child. It is not offered to the general public and does not knowingly solicit personal information from unrelated children.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the reminder service or its messaging practices change. The effective date shown at the top of the page will be updated when material changes are made.
