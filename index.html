Private household reminder program

Helpful reminders for everyday family routines.

Schreiber Home Reminders is a private, consent-based SMS program operated by Mike Schreiber for members of one household. It sends non-marketing reminders and processes simple replies.

Privacy commitment

Mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, and messaging consent information are not shared, sold, rented, transferred, or otherwise provided to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Messaging information

  • Reply STOP to cancel.
