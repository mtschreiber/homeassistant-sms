Terms and Conditions

Messaging consent, frequency, commands, opt-out rights, and program terms.

Effective date: July 14, 2026

These terms govern participation in the Mike Schreiber Home Reminders SMS program.

1. Program description

Mike Schreiber Home Reminders, operated by Mike Schreiber, sends recurring, non-marketing household routine and dental retainer reminders to recipients who independently enroll by texting START to the published Twilio number.

2. Consent

By texting START, you affirmatively consent to receive recurring automated SMS messages from Mike Schreiber Home Reminders. Consent is voluntary and is not a condition of purchasing any product or service. Consent applies only to this messaging program and is not transferred to another person or organization.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies and may include up to 3 messages per day while a reminder remains unconfirmed. Messages may include enrollment confirmation, routine reminders, follow-up reminders, completion acknowledgments, help responses, and opt-out confirmation.

4. Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply according to your wireless plan. You are responsible for charges imposed by your mobile carrier.

5. Commands

  • Text START to enroll.
  • Text DONE to confirm completion where supported.
  • Text HELP for assistance.
  • Text STOP to cancel future automated messages.

6. Opt-out

You may cancel at any time by replying STOP. After the opt-out is processed, no further automated reminder messages will be sent unless you later enroll again by texting START.

7. Help and support

Reply HELP for assistance or email mschrib@gmail.com.

8. Privacy

Your information is handled according to the Privacy Policy. Mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, and messaging consent information are not shared, sold, rented, transferred, or otherwise provided to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

9. Delivery and availability

Delivery depends on mobile carriers, telecommunications networks, Twilio, Home Assistant, internet access, and other technical systems. Messages may be delayed, filtered, duplicated, or undelivered.

10. No emergency use

This program is not an emergency, medical, safety, or security notification service and must not be relied on for urgent communications.

11. Appropriate use

This program is used only for lawful, consent-based household reminders. It is not used for advertising, sales, affiliate marketing, lead generation, lending, age-gated content, or third-party promotions.

12. Contact

Questions may be directed to Mike Schreiber at mschrib@gmail.com.
